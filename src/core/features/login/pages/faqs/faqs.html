Access & Download Issues

How can I access the online ALP course?

You can access the online course from the ITSP Mobile App, available on Android, or ALP Web Portal at http://www.alp.punjab.gov.pk

How do I download the ALP mobile app?

You can access the online course from the ITSP Mobile App, available on Android, or ALP Web Portal at http://www.alp.punjab.gov.pk

What do I do if I am unable to download the app?

Check your storage. You are required to have at least 26MB storage for the ALP app to download. Additionally, check that you have a stable internet connection and a compatible software.
